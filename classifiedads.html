I'm Julia and I am starting a microschool in the neighborhood.

It is through a company called Prenda and it's a very exciting new format, especially for children that are not feeling especially successful in a traditional school environment. It is rather like a cross between homeschool and traditional school. The school is actually located in my home, so it is a comfortable environment. Children receive personalized one-on-one attention, but also have opportunities for socializing and working collaboratively with peers.  

Prenda believes in creating empowered, curious, and self-motivated learners. It is also a model that is built on mastery learning as opposed to traditional curriculum pacing that moves forward whether students truly learned the content or not.

Class sizes are very small, between 5-10 students and our school day runs from 9:00 a.m. to 2:00 pm Monday through Thursday. Our first day of school will be in the new year on January 18th.
